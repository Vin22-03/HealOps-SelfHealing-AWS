About HealOps

HealOps is a cloud-native reliability project that demonstrates how modern AWS systems detect failures, recover automatically, and maintain application continuity without manual intervention. The goal is simple â€” show cloud resilience in action.

What this project demonstrates

HealOps captures the real behavior of AWS-managed infrastructure. Whenever a container stops, becomes unhealthy, or fails during deployment, AWS emits events describing what happened. These events are recorded, analyzed, and displayed in a clear incident timeline.

When a failure occurs, the ECS service automatically launches a replacement task to maintain availability. HealOps observes this recovery and measures the time it took from detection to restoration â€” giving a transparent view of system behavior during disruptions.

Nothing is artificially generated. Failures are real, detection is event-driven, recovery is performed by AWS, and metrics like MTTR are calculated from real-world timestamps.
